FAQ

How are prices collected?

Prices are collected through direct checks, pub menus, public information, and community submissions.

How often are prices updated?

Prices are updated whenever new information is available. Each price includes a last verified date where possible.

How do the rankings work?

The main ranking is based on the standard pint price. Lower prices rank higher for best value.

Can pubs suggest updates?

Yes. If a price, happy hour deal, or listing is wrong, please get in touch and I’ll update it.

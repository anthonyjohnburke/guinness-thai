Guinness Bangkok FAQ

Questions about Guinness prices in Bangkok? Guinness Thailand tracks pint prices, happy hour deals and pub listings across the city to help people find better value Guinness in Bangkok.

Why was Guinness Thailand created?

After noticing some pretty wide differences in Guinness prices across Bangkok, I started tracking pubs, happy hour deals and pint prices to help the fine people of Bangkok find the best value Guinness around the city. One pub would charge ฿250, another ฿400. The tracker started as a little side project and slowly turned into Bangkok’s unofficial Guinness economy monitor. Let’s get those prices down!

How are prices collected?

Prices are collected through direct checks, pub menus, public information, and community submissions.

How often are prices updated?

Prices are updated whenever new information is available. Each price includes a last verified date where possible.

How do the Guinness rankings work?

Rankings are mainly based on standard pint prices, with happy hour Guinness deals tracked separately. Lower prices generally rank higher for value.

Can pubs suggest updates?

Yes. If a price, happy hour deal, or listing is wrong, please get in touch and I’ll update it.

Where can I find the cheapest Guinness in Bangkok?

The tracker highlights some of the cheapest Guinness pints currently available across Bangkok, including happy hour deals and promotions.

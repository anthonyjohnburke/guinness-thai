Someone had to do it

I’m Irish, so I do love a proper pint of Guinness. You know the one. Creamy head, branded glass, and a pour that isn’t rushed. In Bangkok and around Thailand, it can be hit and miss. Sometimes you find a pint at a fair price. Other times… not so much.

After spending more baht than I’d like to admit chasing a decent pint around the land of smiles, I started keeping track of where it’s actually worth ordering. This site is a simple attempt to map it all out. No big agenda here, just a guide to help you find a pint of Guinness in Thailand without overpaying. Corcaigh abú!
